Differentiation equations

  • y = sin kx, dy/dx = k cos kx
  • y = cos kx, dy/dx = -k sin kx
  • y = ekx, dy/dx = kekx
  • y = ln x, dy/dx = 1/x
  • y = akx, dy/dx = akxk ln(a)
  • y = tan kx, dy/dx = k sec2 kx
  • y = cosec ks, dy/dx = -k cosec kx cot kx
  • y = sec kx, dy/dx = k sec kx tan kx
  • y = cot kx, dy/dx = -k cosec2 kx

Differentiation rules

  • Chain rule
    dy/dx = dy/du x du/dx
  • Product rule
    y = uv, dy/dx = u(dv/dx) + v(du/dx)
  • Quotient rule
    y = u/v, dy/dx = (v(du/dx) - u(dv/dx))/(v2)
